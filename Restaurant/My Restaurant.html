About Fred's Bizkit Shoppe:
We offer the finest biscuits from
a large variety of different levels
of firmness.

It's all about the he says she sa--
...er It's all about the consistency!

Our choices of biscuit consistency include:

  • Limp:

    Lowest level of baking time.
    For those who are Limpin' with the Bizkit.


  • Medium:

    Baked in the oven for 20 minutes.
    The best beginner's choice.


  • Firm:

    Baked for 28 minutes.
    For those who want a crunch.


  • Rockship:

    Baked for 40 minutes.
    Practically burnt, and your teeth will pop off the Rockship.
